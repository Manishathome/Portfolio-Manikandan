04 / Case Study

Deep
Dive

Real problems, structured solutions, and measurable outcomes from the field.

Workflow Transformation

LQA Workflow Optimization System

Problem
Manual, ad hoc tracking caused delays, missed issues, and no visibility into the status of QA resolutions across multiple languages and linguists. Stakeholders had no reliable picture of delivery readiness.
Action
Designed and implemented a structured LQA tracking system categorizing errors by type and severity. Established consistent follow-up workflows with clear ownership, escalation paths, and closure criteria. Automated status reporting with Apps Script.
Outcome
Transformed reactive firefighting into a proactive governance model. Visibility and accountability increased across every active language pair and project stream.
Measurable Results
  • Improved LQA issue resolution speed — faster identification, faster linguist response, faster sign-off.
  • Reduced delivery delays caused by unresolved QA issues entering the final review stage.
  • Increased stakeholder visibility with real-time dashboards replacing manual status emails.
  • Built and maintained KPI dashboards in Google Sheets tracking TAT, SLA adherence, defect rates, and vendor performance across 30+ languages.
  • Zero SLA breaches maintained across 12+ consecutive months of managed projects.
Technical Initiative

iVakya LQA Tracker — Real-Time Sync

Problem
QA data lived in disconnected spreadsheets, making it impossible to get a consolidated view of issue status, linguist performance, or delivery risk across the team of 100+ linguists.
Action
Developed a database-backed LQA tracking solution using Supabase for real-time synchronization. Built a structured data model for issue logging, linguist feedback cycles, and resolution tracking that served as the single source of truth.
Outcome
Gave the team a live operational picture for the first time. Reporting speed improved significantly, and the data foundation now supports continuous quality improvement decisions.
Key Impact
  • Real-time data visibility for a team of 100+ linguists and project stakeholders.
  • Eliminated siloed spreadsheets — one source of truth for all QA data.
  • Improved reporting speed and accuracy, enabling faster decisions and proactive risk mitigation.
